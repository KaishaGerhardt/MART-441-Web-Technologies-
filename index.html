Who is Kaisha?

Kaisha Gerhardt: A femme fatale, but the 19th-century French definition, which is literally “disastrous woman”.

However, she is mostly an artist and a proud mom of two cats.

She is currently working towards a BFA in Media Arts with a focus on Game Design. Alongside minoring in Business Administration

She has recently been working with the Milwaukee Railroad Historical Association on creating a book about Taft, Montana.

She is the graphic designer for the book has created the front and back cover, maps, and multiple illustrations.

The book is scheduled to be published in 2022.

Kaisha’s most recent work opportunity is working a paid internship with Open AIR (a place-based Artist-in-Residence program) for the 2022 year.

Kaisha is a proud parent to her two cats named Chewy and Cha-Chi.

Twin brothers who are spunky, sassy, and over the weight limit.

Art Work

Kaisha works with multiple medians and now is dabbling in the world of animation.

Most of her art pieces represent emotions not that can not be well expressed.

She hopes to take her love of art into game design and branch off into character design and backgrounds.
